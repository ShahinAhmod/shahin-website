About Me

Hello, I’m Shahin Ahmod. I’m passionate about Accounting, Finance, and Artificial Intelligence. I’ve built this platform to help enthusiasts learn, grow, and stay up to date with financial education and real-world tools.

I hold a Bachelor’s in Accounting & Finance and an MBA from Tallinn University of Technology. I’m currently working as a PFA Analyst at Ericsson, Estonia.

This website is not just a learning space — it's a community hub for future finance experts.

In the next phase, I will be offering interactive lessons, downloadable resources, and guidance for those who want to master the fundamentals and stay future-ready.

Thanks for visiting! Let's build something meaningful together.
