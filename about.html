About Me

I’m Shahin Ahmod, a finance enthusiast with a strong background in Accounting and an MBA from Tallinn University of Technology. I’ve worked in companies like KFC, Stora Enso, and Ericsson, where I was promoted within three months.

My goal is to share practical knowledge in Accounting and Finance to empower students, professionals, and small businesses. Through this platform, I aim to deliver hands-on courses, financial tools, and blog content to simplify finance and boost your confidence in numbers.
